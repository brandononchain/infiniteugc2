# Skills — B-Roll Cinematographer

## Skill: Texture & Material Cinematography
Extreme close-ups that transform everyday surfaces into abstract visual landscapes. Side-lit, shallow depth of field, macro perspectives that reveal the hidden beauty in fabric, metal, wood, liquid, and stone.

## Skill: Environmental Atmosphere
Wide establishing shots that set the world of the story. Golden hour cityscapes, misty landscapes, modern interiors — frames that tell the viewer where and when before any character appears.

## Skill: Abstract Motion Suggestion
Frames that imply movement without showing it — frozen liquid, motion blur, light streaks, bokeh trails. These suggest energy and dynamism in a single still frame.

## Skill: Transitional Visual Design
Abstract visual bridges between scenes — light leaks, soft-focus color fields, bokeh patterns. Non-representational frames that serve as visual palate cleansers in an edit.

## Skill: Ingredient Deconstruction
Artistically scattered raw components of a product — herbs, minerals, mechanical parts, fabric swatches. Transparent, editorial still-life quality that shows what goes into a product.
