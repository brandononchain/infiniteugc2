# Templates — Storyboard Keyframe Artist

## template: ugc-ad-storyboard
**Name**: UGC Ad Storyboard (5 frames)
**Description**: Visual storyboard for a standard UGC product advertisement
**Tags**: storyboard, ugc, ad, product, sequence
**Keyframes**: 5
**Prompt**:
- FRAME 1 [HOOK]: Wide shot of a person in a bright modern bathroom/kitchen, looking directly at camera with expressive "I need to tell you something" energy, natural window light, warm tones, 24mm wide lens, authentic UGC feel
- FRAME 2 [PROBLEM]: Medium shot of the same person gesturing with hands expressing frustration, slightly dimmer lighting suggesting the "problem" state, same environment but tighter framing, 50mm lens, relatable and genuine expression
- FRAME 3 [DISCOVERY]: Close-up of hands holding and presenting the product, product label visible and lit beautifully, soft focused background showing the same environment, rim light on the product edges, 85mm lens, moment of revelation
- FRAME 4 [DEMO]: Medium-close shot of the person actively using the product, visible satisfaction on their face, bright warm lighting returning, product and its effect clearly visible, 50mm lens, demonstrative and confident energy
- FRAME 5 [CTA]: Medium-wide shot of the person holding the product near their face, direct eye contact with camera, warm golden lighting, clean background with the product as hero, confident smile, 35mm lens, branded and inviting

## template: cinematic-hero-storyboard
**Name**: Cinematic Product Launch Storyboard (6 frames)
**Description**: Premium storyboard for a cinematic product reveal sequence
**Tags**: storyboard, cinematic, product, launch, premium
**Keyframes**: 6
**Prompt**:
- FRAME 1 [ATMOSPHERE]: Ultra-wide establishing shot of an abstract dark environment, mist and volumetric light, deep shadows with a single beam of warm light breaking through, no subject visible yet, mysterious and anticipatory, 24mm anamorphic, film grain
- FRAME 2 [DETAIL]: Extreme macro close-up of a surface texture — brushed metal, glass, or material detail, shallow depth of field, side lighting raking across revealing micro-detail, we cannot identify what it is yet, abstract and beautiful, 100mm macro
- FRAME 3 [REVEAL]: Medium shot — the product emerging from darkness as camera pulls back, rim light defining its silhouette, the form becoming recognizable, dramatic shadows, volumetric haze, 50mm, moment of recognition
- FRAME 4 [HERO]: The beauty shot — full product in frame at the hero angle, orbiting light that catches every surface, deep dark background, reflections on a glossy surface below, every detail sharp and premium, 70mm f/2.0, the definitive view
- FRAME 5 [CONTEXT]: The product in its world — a hand reaching toward it, or placed in an aspirational environment, warm but controlled lighting, the brand promise visualized through environment, 35mm, human connection
- FRAME 6 [LOCKUP]: Clean brand end frame — product small in frame with vast dark negative space, elegant typography zone above and below, minimal, confident, 50mm, the final impression

## template: story-arc-storyboard
**Name**: Story-Driven Narrative Storyboard (7 frames)
**Description**: Full emotional narrative arc with cinematic progression
**Tags**: storyboard, story, narrative, emotional, arc
**Keyframes**: 7
**Prompt**:
- FRAME 1 [ORDINARY WORLD]: Wide establishing shot of the protagonist's world before the change, muted but warm colors, everyday environment, soft overcast lighting, 24mm, normal and relatable but slightly unsatisfying
- FRAME 2 [INCITING INCIDENT]: Medium shot of the moment everything changes — the discovery, the problem revealed, the realization, dramatic shift in lighting or color, 50mm, the turning point
- FRAME 3 [STRUGGLE]: Close-up showing effort, frustration, or vulnerability, tight framing creating tension, shadows deeper, color temperature cooling, 85mm, the emotional low point
- FRAME 4 [DISCOVERY]: Medium shot of finding the solution/product, a beam of warm light in a darker scene, hope entering the frame, product introduced naturally, 50mm, the glimmer of possibility
- FRAME 5 [TRANSFORMATION]: Dynamic medium-wide shot showing the change in progress, lighting dramatically warmer, colors saturating, energy visible in the composition — diagonals, movement implied, 35mm, momentum building
- FRAME 6 [NEW WORLD]: Wide shot mirroring frame 1 but transformed — same environment now brighter, warmer, more alive, the protagonist's world improved, 24mm, satisfying visual payoff
- FRAME 7 [RESOLUTION]: Clean medium shot of the protagonist at peace, product naturally present, warm golden light, direct connection with camera, 50mm, earned confidence and genuine contentment
