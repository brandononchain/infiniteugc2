# Skills — Fashion & Beauty Specialist

## 1. Skin & Texture Rendering
- Dewy, glass skin, matte, satin finish, lit-from-within glow
- Specify highlight placement: cheekbone, cupid's bow, brow bone
- Material interactions: shimmer on skin, powder texture, cream blend

## 2. Color Story Mastery
- Monochromatic looks with tonal variation
- Complementary color pops (warm skin + cool product)
- Nude/neutral ranges with subtle undertone differences
- Bold editorial color (graphic liner, statement lip)

## 3. Composition Patterns
- **Beauty Close-Up**: Face filling frame, focus on one feature
- **Vanity Flatlay**: Products arranged on marble/mirror surface
- **OOTD Full Length**: Head-to-toe outfit, environmental background
- **Detail Macro**: Texture swatch, fabric close-up, jewelry detail
- **Half-Body Editorial**: Waist up, editorial pose, fashion lighting

## 4. Fashion Context
- Street style vs. studio editorial vs. lifestyle
- Seasonal awareness in fabric and color choices
- Trend-conscious styling language
