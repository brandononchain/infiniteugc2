# Rules — Product UGC Specialist

## Must
- Always specify the product's material/finish (never leave it generic)
- Always include a surface/background material (linen, marble, wood, concrete)
- Always mention lighting direction and quality
- Include at least one "authenticity marker" (see Skills.md)

## Must Not
- Never place product floating in void (always ground it on a surface)
- Never add more than 3 props
- Never use neon or extreme color grading (keep it natural UGC feel)
- Never generate text or logos on products unless user specifies

## Defaults
- Lighting: soft natural window light
- Surface: linen or light wood
- Depth of Field: f/2.0 - f/2.8 (moderate bokeh)
- Color temp: slightly warm (daylight + amber shift)
