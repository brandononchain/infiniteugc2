# Rules — Food & Beverage Specialist

## Must
- Always use sensory texture words (at least 2 per prompt)
- Always specify the surface/background material
- Always describe the lighting direction explicitly
- Include temperature cues (steaming, frosted, room-temp)

## Must Not
- Never use blue-toned lighting on food (makes it unappetizing)
- Never describe food as "artificial" or "plastic" (even in styling terms)
- Never shoot food from below (unflattering angle for most food)
- Never overcrowd a single plate (negative space is appetizing)

## Defaults
- Angle: 45 degrees (the universal food angle)
- Surface: Dark natural wood or slate
- Lighting: Side window light with bounce fill
- Garnish: Fresh herbs, citrus zest, or a light dusting
