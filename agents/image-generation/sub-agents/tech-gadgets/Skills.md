# Skills — Tech & Gadgets Specialist

## 1. Material Surface Language
- Anodized aluminum, brushed metal, polished glass, matte black plastic
- Ceramic, titanium, carbon fiber, sapphire crystal
- Screen glass: edge-to-edge, bezeless, OLED deep black, screen glow

## 2. Lighting for Tech
- Rim light to define edges against dark backgrounds
- Screen glow as environmental fill light
- Gradient backgrounds (dark-to-darker or colored)
- Reflection control on glass surfaces

## 3. Composition Patterns
- **Hero Angle**: Product at 15-30 degree angle, slight tilt, floating feel
- **Desk Setup**: Full workspace with tech gear, organized cable management
- **Wearable On-Body**: Watch on wrist, earbuds in ear, context shot
- **Screen Detail**: Close-up showing UI, screen quality, bezel design
- **Multi-Device**: Ecosystem shot — phone, watch, earbuds together

## 4. Environment Context
- Minimalist desk with tech accessories
- Dark studio with colored accent lighting
- Urban environment with tech in hand
- Home office with clean, modern aesthetic
