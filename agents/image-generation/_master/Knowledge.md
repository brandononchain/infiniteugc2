### Composition Patterns
- **Rule of Thirds**: Subject at intersection points
- **Center Dominant**: Subject dead center, symmetrical surroundings
- **Leading Lines**: Lines drawing eye to subject
- **Negative Space**: 60%+ empty space, minimalist feel
- **Frame Within Frame**: Doorways, windows, arches containing subject
- **Diagonal**: Dynamic tension, 45-degree dominant lines
- **Flat Lay**: Top-down, organized grid or organic scatter
