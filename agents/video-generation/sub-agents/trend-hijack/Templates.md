# Templates — Trend Hijack Specialist

## template: pov-format
**Name**: POV Trend
**Description**: Classic POV format adapted for product/brand content
**Tags**: trend, pov, viral, format
**Duration**: 15s
**Script**:
[TEXT OVERLAY - full duration] "POV: You finally found a [product category] that actually works"
[BEAT 1 - 3s] [Skeptical face / looking at product]
[BEAT 2 - 3s] [Trying the product, slight surprise]
[BEAT 3 - 4s] [Clear reaction — the moment it works, visible satisfaction]
[BEAT 4 - 3s] [Looking at camera like "yeah, this is it" — smug/satisfied]
[END - 2s] [Hold product up to camera with a knowing nod]
**Note**: Match to a trending sound with a build-up beat drop structure

## template: expectation-reality
**Name**: Expectation vs Reality
**Description**: Humorous comparison subverted to showcase product quality
**Tags**: trend, humor, comparison, viral
**Duration**: 20s
**Script**:
[TEXT: "Expectation vs Reality"]
[PART 1 - 5s] "What I expected when I ordered [product] online:" [Show exaggerated low expectations — basic, disappointing version]
[TRANSITION - 2s] [Quick cut / beat drop]
[PART 2 - 5s] "What I actually got:" [Show the product looking amazing, better than expected]
[REACTION - 3s] [Genuine surprise reaction — raised eyebrows, looking at camera]
[END - 5s] [Text overlay: product name + "link in bio" + engaging question like "has an online order ever exceeded your expectations?"]

## template: this-or-that
**Name**: This or That Challenge
**Description**: Quick comparison format with dramatic choice reveal
**Tags**: trend, challenge, engagement, interactive
**Duration**: 15s
**Script**:
[SETUP - 3s] "Let's settle this once and for all." [TEXT: "This or That"]
[OPTION A - 3s] [Show option A — the competitor/old way] [TEXT: current approach]
[OPTION B - 3s] [Show option B — the product] [TEXT: the product name]
[CHOICE - 3s] [Dramatic pause...] [Confidently choose option B]
[SEAL - 3s] [TEXT: "and it's not even close" + brief reason why]
**Note**: End with "Comment your pick" for engagement

## template: storytime-quick

[CTA - 3s] [TEXT: "Stitch this with YOUR storytime"]
