# Skills — Trend Hijack Specialist

## 1. Trend Format Adaptation
- Identify the core structure of any trending format
- Map brand/product messaging onto the format's template
- Maintain the format's energy and timing (deviation = cringe)
- Add brand personality without breaking the format

## 2. Sound/Music Integration
- Reference trending audio patterns (not specific copyrighted tracks)
- Match script pacing to beat drops and transitions
- Use sound as a structural element (spoken word, beat switch, dramatic pause)

## 3. Viral Mechanics
- Loop-worthy endings (last frame connects to first)
- Shareable moments (screenshot-worthy frames)
- Comment-bait lines ("wait for it", "which one are you?")
- Duet/stitch-friendly openings (invite responses)

## 4. Trend Format Templates
- **POV Format**: "POV: [scenario]" + visual demonstration
- **This or That**: Two options compared, dramatic reveal of choice
- **Get Ready With Me**: Routine format with product integration
- **Expectation vs Reality**: Split-screen or before/after with humor
- **"Tell me without telling me"**: Show-don't-tell challenge format
- **Storytime**: Quick personal narrative with dramatic beats

## 5. Cultural Fluency
- Reference current internet culture without forcing it
- Self-aware humor > trying too hard
- Know when a trend is past peak (don't be late)
- Subvert expectations when possible (the twist gets shares)
