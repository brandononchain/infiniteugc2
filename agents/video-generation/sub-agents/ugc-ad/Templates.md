# Templates — UGC Ad Specialist

## template: classic-ugc-ad
**Name**: Classic UGC Product Ad
**Description**: Standard problem → solution UGC ad format
**Tags**: ugc, ad, product, conversion
**Structure**: Hook → Problem → Discovery → Demo → CTA
**Duration**: 30s
**Script**:
[HOOK - 3s] "Okay I literally need to tell you about this because I wish someone told ME sooner..."
[PROBLEM - 5s] "So I've been struggling with [problem] for months, tried everything, nothing worked..."
[DISCOVERY - 5s] "Then I found [product] and honestly? I'm kind of mad I didn't find it earlier."
[DEMO - 10s] "Look — [show product]. You just [simple usage]. And the [key benefit]? *chef's kiss*"
[CTA - 7s] "I put the link in my bio. Seriously, if you've been dealing with [problem], you need this. They're running [offer] right now but I don't know how long it's gonna last."
