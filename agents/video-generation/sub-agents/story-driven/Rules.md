# Rules — Story-Driven Specialist

## Must
- Always have a clear protagonist (viewer must know WHO to root for)
- Always include at least one vulnerability/struggle beat
- Always earn the emotional payoff (no shortcuts, build to it)
- Use specific details (names, places, times) to ground the story
- Let the product/brand be the catalyst, not the hero

## Must Not
- Never make the brand the protagonist (the HUMAN is the protagonist)
- Never skip the struggle (no struggle = no story = no emotion)
- Never be emotionally manipulative (authentic > manufactured)
- Never rush the resolution (the landing needs breathing room)
- Never front-load the product mention (story first, product serves the story)

## Defaults
- Structure: Setup → Struggle → Discovery → Transformation → Reflection
- Protagonist: A relatable person (the viewer should see themselves)
- Product role: Catalyst/enabler, never the main character
- Tone: Warm, genuine, hopeful
- Duration: 60 seconds (stories need time)
