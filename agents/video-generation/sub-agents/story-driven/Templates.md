# Templates — Story-Driven Specialist

## template: transformation-journey
**Name**: Transformation Journey
**Description**: Full transformation arc from struggle to triumph
**Tags**: story, transformation, emotional, journey
**Duration**: 60s
**Script**:
[SETUP - 5s] "[Quiet, reflective tone] Six months ago, I was [describe the before state]. I remember [specific moment that captures the struggle]."
[STRUGGLE - 10s] "I tried everything. [Specific attempt 1], [attempt 2]. I spent [time/money]. Nothing worked. And honestly? I was starting to think this was just... how it was gonna be."
[DISCOVERY - 8s] "Then [how they found the product]. I almost didn't [buy it / try it] because [honest doubt]. But something made me go for it."
[EARLY DAYS - 8s] "First couple weeks? [Honest early experience]. Not dramatic. Just... [small first sign]. Enough to keep going."
[TURNING POINT - 8s] "Then one morning, [the moment]. I [specific observation]. And I realized — this is actually working. [Emotional reaction]."
[NOW - 8s] "[Warmer, more confident tone] Now? [Describe the after state]. It's not perfect. But [specific measurable/visible change]. And I feel [emotion]."
[REFLECTION - 5s] "If you're where I was [timeframe] ago — just know it gets better. You just need [the right thing]."
[SOFT CTA - 3s] "[Product name] is linked below. That's my story. I hope it helps."

## template: day-in-the-life
**Name**: Day in the Life
**Description**: Dawn-to-dusk story with product naturally woven in
**Tags**: story, daily, lifestyle, routine
**Duration**: 45s
**Script**:
[DAWN - 5s] "[Soft, just-woke-up energy] 5:47 AM. Alarm goes off. Today's a big day — [context for what's happening today]."
[MORNING - 8s] "First thing: [morning routine moment with product naturally present]. This has become my [ritual/non-negotiable]. [Why it matters to the morning]."
[MIDDAY - 8s] "[Energy picks up] By noon, I'm [activity]. This is where [product benefit becomes relevant]. Before I had this, I used to [old way]. Now [new way]. The difference is [tangible]."
[AFTERNOON - 7s] "[Quick montage energy] [Activity], [activity], [activity]. Through all of it — [product is there, doing its thing]. It just... works in the background."
[EVENING - 7s] "[Winding down energy] End of the day. [Reflective moment]. I think what I love about [product] is that it's not loud. It's just... reliable. It fits."
[CLOSE - 5s] "[Looking at camera, genuine] If you want a day that flows like this — I put everything I use in my bio. [Product name] is the one that changed things."

## template: behind-the-scenes
**Name**: Behind the Scenes
**Description**: The making-of, the craft, the process behind a product
**Tags**: story, bts, process, authentic
**Duration**: 45s
**Script**:
[OPEN - 5s] "Most people see the finished [product]. But they don't see this part."
[PROCESS - 10s] "[Show/describe the making process]. Every [unit] takes [time/effort]. [Specific detail about craftsmanship]. [Another detail]. It's not the fastest way. But it's the right way."
[CHALLENGE - 8s] "The hardest part is [honest challenge in the process]. There are days when [struggle]. When [setback]. But then [what keeps them going]."
[DETAIL - 7s] "[Close-up moment] See this? [Point out a small detail most people miss]. That's [time/effort] of [process]. Most [competitors] skip this step. We don't."
[WHY - 7s] "We do it this way because [belief/value]. Because [what the customer deserves]. Because [mission]."
[CLOSE - 3s] "That's what's behind every [product]. Now you know."

## template: founder-origin
**Name**: Founder Origin Story
**Description**: Why the brand/product was created — the human story behind it
**Tags**: story, founder, origin, mission
**Duration**: 60s
**Script**:
[THE BEFORE - 8s] "Before [brand name], I was [previous life/job]. Doing [activity]. And every day I dealt with [the problem that inspired the product]."
[THE MOMENT - 8s] "One day, [the inciting incident — the specific moment that sparked the idea]. I remember thinking: why does nobody fix this? Why is every option [current market failure]?"
[THE BEGINNING - 10s] "So I [first action]. It was terrible. [laugh/honest self-deprecation]. The first version was [funny/humble description]. But [someone / the first user] tried it and said [reaction that validated the idea]."
[THE STRUGGLE - 8s] "The next [time period] was [honest about the difficulty]. [Specific setback]. [Another setback]. There were moments I almost stopped. My [person close to them] told me [encouragement or honest concern]."
[THE BREAKTHROUGH - 8s] "Then [the turning point]. We [specific achievement — first sale, first customer, first real product]. And I knew this was real."
[NOW - 5s] "That was [time period] ago. Now [where the brand is today]. But every time I [interact with the product], I still think about [that original problem]."
[MISSION - 3s] "That's why [brand name] exists. Not because the world needed another [category]. Because [the real reason]."
