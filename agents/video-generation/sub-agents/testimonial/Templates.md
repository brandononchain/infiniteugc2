## template: honest-review
**Name**: Honest Product Review
**Description**: Balanced, credible product review with genuine reactions
**Tags**: testimonial, review, honest, trust
**Duration**: 45s
**Script**:
[SETUP - 5s] "Okay so I've been using [product] for [timeframe] now, and I have thoughts. Honest ones."
[SKEPTICISM - 7s] "When I first heard about it, I was honestly pretty skeptical. I'd already tried [alternative 1] and [alternative 2], and neither really worked for me. So I wasn't expecting much."
[EXPERIENCE - 10s] "But I started using it [how], and after about [timeframe], I noticed [specific change]. Like, actually noticeable. The [specific detail] was the first thing — then [second observation]."
[CAVEAT - 5s] "Now, is it perfect? No. I will say [minor negative or wish]. That's my one thing. But honestly..."
[VERDICT - 8s] "For what it does? And especially at [price point]? Yeah. I'm keeping this one. It's become part of my [routine/daily use] and I genuinely recommend it."
[CTA - 5s] "If you've been on the fence, I'd say go for it. Link below."

## template: before-after-journey

**Description**: Transformation story with measurable before/after results
**Tags**: testimonial, transformation, before-after, results
**Duration**: 60s
**Script**:
[BEFORE - 8s] "So [timeframe] ago, I was dealing with [problem]. Like, it was bad. I was [specific impact on life]. I'd tried basically everything."
[DISCOVERY - 5s] "A friend mentioned [product] and I was like, sure, why not, I'll try one more thing."
[EARLY - 8s] "First [time period], I'm gonna be real — I didn't notice much. I almost stopped. But I kept going because [reason]."
[TURNING POINT - 8s] "Then around [time period], something shifted. I [specific first sign of change]. And then [second change]. I literally took a photo because I couldn't believe it."

[CTA - 5s] "Link in the description if you want to try it. Just be patient with it — give it [time period]."
