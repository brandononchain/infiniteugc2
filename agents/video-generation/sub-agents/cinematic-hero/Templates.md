# Templates — Cinematic Hero Specialist

## template: product-launch-film
**Name**: Product Launch Film
**Description**: Premium product introduction with cinematic grandeur
**Tags**: cinematic, launch, hero, premium
**Duration**: 45s
**Script**:
[SHOT 1 - 5s] WIDE: Slow pan across an abstract environment. Mist. Light breaking through. Anticipation. The score begins — a single sustained note.
[SHOT 2 - 5s] DETAIL: Extreme close-up of a surface — material texture, light catching edges. We don't know what it is yet. Rack focus.
[SHOT 3 - 5s] REVEAL: Slow dolly back. The product emerges from the detail — its form becoming recognizable. Light wraps around it.
[SHOT 4 - 8s] HERO: Full product in frame. Orbiting camera movement. Every detail visible. The light finds every surface. This is the beauty shot.
[SHOT 5 - 7s] CONTEXT: The product in its world — a hand reaching, an environment that embodies the brand promise.
[SHOT 6 - 5s] CLOSE: Tight on the key detail that makes this product special. Slow push-in.
[END - 5s] FADE: Elegant brand lockup. Name. Tagline. Clean typography on black.

## template: brand-mood-film
**Name**: Brand Mood Film
**Description**: Atmospheric brand world film without explicit product focus
**Tags**: cinematic, mood, brand, atmospheric
**Duration**: 30s
**Script**:
[SHOT 1 - 5s] WIDE: An environment that IS the brand — architecture, nature, or abstraction. Golden light. Slow crane up.
[SHOT 2 - 5s] HUMAN: A figure in this world, moving with purpose. Back-lit. We see the silhouette, not the face. Mystery.
[SHOT 3 - 5s] TEXTURE: Close-up of a material moment — water, fabric, light through glass. Macro beauty.
[SHOT 4 - 5s] MOTION: Slow motion of something beautiful in motion — liquid, hair, fabric in wind. 3x speed.
[SHOT 5 - 5s] LANDING: Pull back to reveal the full scene. Everything clicks. The mood is undeniable.
[END - 5s] TYPE: Brand name fades in. Minimal. Serif. Below it, a single line that captures the ethos.

## template: hero-product-orbit
**Name**: Hero Product Orbit
**Description**: Single product, orbiting camera, pure visual worship
**Tags**: cinematic, product, orbit, minimal
**Duration**: 20s
**Script**:
[SHOT 1 - 5s] BLACK: Darkness. A point of light appears. It grows. We hear a tone — deep and resonant.
[SHOT 2 - 10s] ORBIT: The product sits on a seamless surface. Camera begins a slow 180-degree orbit. Every angle, every material, every detail illuminated by a moving key light that follows the camera. Slow, reverent, hypnotic.
[SHOT 3 - 3s] LOCK: Camera settles on the hero angle. The definitive view. Perfect lighting. Hold.
[END - 2s] BRAND: Name appears below. Nothing else. Confidence.
