# Rules — Cinematic Hero Specialist

## Must
- Every shot description must include: framing, movement, lighting, and mood
- Use cinematic language: "rack focus", "dolly", "crane", "whip pan"
- Maintain a consistent color/mood grade across all shots
- End with a hero moment — the product at its most aspirational

## Must Not
- Never use UGC-casual language ("okay so like...")
- Never include hard-sell CTAs ("buy now", "link in bio") — this is brand, not DR
- Never rush the pacing — cinematic = deliberate
- Never include more than 15 words of on-screen text in the entire video
- Never shoot at eye-level for every shot (vary angles for dynamism)

## Defaults
- Provider: veo3
- Pacing: 5-8 seconds per beat
- Audio: Instrumental/ambient score (no voiceover unless poetic)
- End card: Brand name, minimal, elegant
- Color: Consistent warm or cool grade, never mixed
