# Knowledge Base — Video Master Agent

## Video Provider Capabilities

### heygen
- **Type**: AI Avatar talking head
- **Best for**: Professional spokesperson videos, product explanations
- **Limitations**: Limited to upper body, preset avatars
- **Aspect ratios**: 9:16, 16:9, 1:1

### omnihuman
- **Type**: Full body human generation
- **Best for**: Full-body product demos, lifestyle scenes with people
- **Limitations**: Premium tier only, longer processing
- **Aspect ratios**: 9:16, 16:9

### sora2 / sora2pro
- **Type**: Text-to-video generation
- **Best for**: Creative cinematic content, abstract visuals, scene generation
- **Limitations**: No precise human control, artistic interpretation
- **Aspect ratios**: 9:16, 16:9, 1:1

### seedance
- **Type**: Dance and movement generation
- **Best for**: Dynamic movement content, dance challenges, music videos
- **Limitations**: Movement-focused, less control over dialogue
- **Aspect ratios**: 9:16

### hedra_avatar / hedra_omnia
- **Type**: AI Avatar generation
- **Best for**: Quick avatar content, mass production, talking head at scale
- **Limitations**: avatar = head/shoulders, omnia = more versatile
- **Aspect ratios**: 9:16, 16:9, 1:1

### veo3
- **Type**: High-quality video generation (Google)
- **Best for**: Premium cinematic content, hero videos, brand content
- **Limitations**: Premium tier, slower, higher cost
- **Aspect ratios**: 9:16, 16:9, 1:1

## Platform Best Practices

### TikTok
- Hook in 0-1.5 seconds (you have 300ms before scroll)
- Native feel > polished production
- Trending sounds boost distribution 3-5x
- Loop-worthy endings boost completion rate
- Green screen and duet-friendly formats

### Instagram Reels
- Slightly more polished than TikTok
- Branded content performs well
- 7-15 second Reels get highest completion
- Strong cover frame matters for grid

### YouTube Shorts
- Informational content outperforms entertainment
- "How to" and "Did you know" hooks dominate
- Subscribe CTA in last 3 seconds
- Vertical but can reference longer YouTube content

## Hook Pattern Library

### Curiosity Gap
- "Nobody's talking about this..."
- "I can't believe [brand] is getting away with this"
- "This changed everything about my [routine]"

### Pattern Interrupt
- "STOP. Before you [action], watch this"
- "Wait wait wait — you're still [doing X]?"
- "Delete that app right now. Here's why."

### Social Proof
- "2 million people already switched to this"
- "Why every [profession] is obsessed with this"
- "The thing your favorite creator won't tell you"

### Direct Challenge
- "You've been [doing X] wrong your entire life"
- "If you're still [old way], I feel bad for you"
- "POV: You finally found [the solution]"

### Empathy / Relatability
- "If your [problem], you NEED this"
- "Tell me you [experience] without telling me"
- "This is for my [demographic] who [struggle with X]"
